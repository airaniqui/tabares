Problem and Solution

Isn't it a relief to be able to access the information inside your USB flashdrive without pluggin it in?

Well, Koda-drive could be the solution to your dilemma! The Koda-Drive comes with a small touchscreen display that allows users to see documents without having to plug the USB flash drive into a computer.

Have you lost your USB flash drive or accidentally misplaced it?

Don't you worry! Koda-drive has a built in tracker which enables you to track your lost USB flashdrive.
