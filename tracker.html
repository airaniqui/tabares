Tracker

The Koda-drive has a built in tracker.

USB flash drives are compact, portable storage devices that connect to a computer via a USB interface, which makes them simple to misplace. As a result, the company Flashvision has decided to include a tracker on a USB flash drive so that it may be easily located if it is misplaced.
