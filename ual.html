Users Acceptable Level

Graph and interpretation

1. Is the information complete?
Out of 30 respondents, 100% or all of the respondents answered that the information is complete
2. Is the location of the navigation tools convenient?
Out of 30 respondents, 100% or all of the respondents answered that the location of the navigation tools is convenient.
3. Is there uniqueness in the content?
Out of 30 respondents, 100% or all of the respondents answered that the there is uniqueness in the content of the website.
4. Is the content reliable? (does it produce true information)
Out of 30 respondents, 100% or all of the respondents answered that the content of the website is reliable because it produces true information
5. Is the content readable?
Out of 30 respondents, 100% or all of the respondents answered that the content is readable meaning the font style and sizes that is used in the website is appropriate
6. Is there a site map to guide the viewer?
Out of 30 respondents, 100% or all of the respondents answered that there is a site map that guides the viewer.
7. Is the loading speed fast?
Out of 30 respondents, 100% or all of the respondents answered that the loading speed of the website is fast.
8. Is the information in-time/updted?
Out of 30 respondents, 100% or all of the respondents answered that the information is updated.
9. Is the website compatible to all browser?
Out of 30 respondents, 100% or all of the respondent answered that the website is compatible to their browser meaning that it is compatible in all browser.
10. Is the graphics detailed/clear?
Out of 30 respondents, 100% or all of the respondents answered that the graphics is detailed and clear.
