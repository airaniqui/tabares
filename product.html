Storage Capacity

There are multiple options for the storage capacity of this USB flash drive

4gb

- This USB flash drive is for more moderate use, the 4GB thumb drive is appropriate. Students will frequently use these to store backups of projects and documents, as the 4GB capacity is sufficient for a big amount of such files. It can also be used to transfer and store modest amounts of image, music, and video files.

8gb

- In this variation, you have enough space to store a reasonable number of files. This size can store thousands of image and music files, making it excellent for students, office employees, and those who want to back up several years' worth of records on a single device.

16gb

- This type of flash drive is ideal for storing and transmitting huge data or media files. Users may transfer thousands of music files, as well as video files and other types of data, using these devices.

32gb

- This flash drive capacity is capable of storing thousands of data files, sending massive volumes of high-resolution video files, and storing complete libraries of music and images, among other things. You can also save a number of programs.
